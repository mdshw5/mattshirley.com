MethylString decodes ASCII encoded methylation strings from bisulfite converted DNA sequences.

Output format

Each ASCII character encodes two digits which encode information about two nucleotide positions. Possible values for the methylation string (MT) are:

    0 - not a cytosine
    1 - converted, unmethylated cytosine on top strand
    2 - converted, unmethylated cytosine on bot strand
    3 - converted, unmethylated cytosine on top strand complement
    4 - converted, unmethylated cytosine on bot strand complement
    5 - methylated cytosine on top strand
    6 - methylated cytosine on bot strand
    7 - reserved
    8 - reserved

A representational diagram of top and bottom strands is also produced, which depicts the top and bottom DNA strand as well as deamination (C/c) and methylation (M) status of every cytosine.
